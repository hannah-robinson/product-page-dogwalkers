Our Services

You love your dog, but life is busy, so sometimes you need a little help. H & H Fictional Dog Walkers can walk your dog for or take him or her to the park to play fetch. Make sure your dog gets all the exercise, fresh air, sunshine and attention he or she needs.

Your dog can join us for:

  • Walks
  • Games of fetch in the park
  • Carefully-supervised runs at the beach
  • A trip to the vet with us, paw-holding included

With regular exercise your dog will be:

  • Healthier
  • Happier
  • Less noisy and destructive around the house

Our services are ideal for:

  • People who don't like going outside on cold rainy days
  • Busy professionals
  • Sleep-deprived parents of newborns
  • Older dog-parents who are finding it hard to keep up with their dog's boundless energy
  • People who need some short-term help with dog walking while recovering from illness

About us

We are a family-operated business that loves dogs. We have been caring for dogs for many years. We will love your dog like it was our own.
